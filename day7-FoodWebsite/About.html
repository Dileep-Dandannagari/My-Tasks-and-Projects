Information about our Dishes

1.Pizza
A classic Italian dish consisting of a flattened dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings, baked in an oven.
2.Burger
A sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef), placed inside a sliced bun, often accompanied by lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.
3.Sushi
A Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as raw fish, seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.
4.Tacos
A traditional Mexican dish made of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients, such as seasoned meat, beans, lettuce, and cheese.
5.Pasta Carbonara
An Italian pasta dish made with egg, hard cheese (usually Pecorino Romano), pancetta, and black pepper, typically combined with spaghetti.
6.Steak
A slice of high-quality meat (such as beef) cut perpendicular to the muscle fibers, usually grilled, pan-fried, or broiled.
7.Sushi Rolls
Similar to sushi but wrapped in seaweed (nori) and typically containing a combination of ingredients like raw fish, vegetables, and rice.
8.Chicken Curry
A dish originating from the Indian subcontinent consisting of chicken pieces cooked in a spiced sauce, typically made with a mixture of onion, tomato, ginger, garlic, and various spices.
9.Caesar Salad
A salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice, olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
10.Lasagna
An Italian pasta dish consisting of layers of wide flat noodles, meat sauce, cheese, and often other ingredients such as vegetables.
11.Sushi Nigiri
A type of sushi made of thin slices of raw fish (or other seafood) served over pressed vinegared rice.
12.Pad Thai
A stir-fried noodle dish from Thailand made with rice noodles, eggs, tofu or shrimp, bean sprouts, peanuts, and a combination of sauces.
13.Hamburger
A sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef) placed inside a sliced bun, often accompanied by lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.
14.Miso Soup
A traditional Japanese soup consisting of a stock called "dashi" into which softened miso paste is mixed, often containing ingredients such as tofu, seaweed, and green onions.
15.Fish and Chips
A British dish consisting of battered and deep-fried fish (usually cod or haddock) accompanied by deep-fried potato slices (chips) and often served with malt vinegar.
16.Spaghetti Bolognese
An Italian pasta dish consisting of spaghetti topped with a meat-based sauce containing ground meat, tomatoes, garlic, and herbs.
17.Pho
A Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat (usually beef or chicken), often served with bean sprouts, lime, and chili peppers.
18.Ramen
A Japanese noodle soup dish consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and toppings such as sliced pork, dried seaweed, and green onions.
19.Pancakes
A flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk, and butter, typically fried and served with syrup, butter, fruit, or other toppings.
20.Tiramisu
An Italian dessert consisting of layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, cocoa powder, and sometimes liqueur.
21.Chicken Tikka Masala
A popular Indian dish consisting of roasted chunks of chicken (tikka) in a spiced tomato-based curry sauce.
22.Falafel
A Middle Eastern dish made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, shaped into balls or patties, and deep-fried, often served in a pita bread with lettuce, tomato, and tahini sauce.
23.Chow Mein
A Chinese stir-fried noodle dish made with wheat noodles, meat (such as chicken, beef, or shrimp), vegetables, and a savory sauce.
24.Guacamole
A Mexican dip made from mashed avocados mixed with lime juice, salt, chopped onion, tomatoes, chili peppers, and cilantro.
25.Croissant
A buttery, flaky, crescent-shaped pastry of Austrian origin, made of layered yeast-leavened dough.
26.Chicken Wings
Chicken wings that are usually fried and then coated or dipped in a sauce, typically served as an appetizer.
27.Ratatouille
A French Provencal stewed vegetable dish, originating from Nice, containing tomatoes, zucchini, eggplant, bell peppers, onions, garlic, and herbs.
28.Tandoori Chicken
A popular Indian dish consisting of roasted chicken marinated in yogurt and seasoned with tandoori masala, a spice blend.
29.Gyoza
Japanese dumplings filled with ground meat and vegetables, wrapped in a thin dough and pan-fried.
30.Pav Bhaji
An Indian street food dish consisting of a spicy mixture of mashed vegetables (such as potatoes, peas, and tomatoes) served with buttered bread rolls.
